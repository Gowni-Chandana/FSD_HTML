Introduction of Fruits

Fruits are the sweet, fleshy, edible parts of flowering plants that typically contain seeds, providing a rich source of vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants, often enjoyed fresh and coming in a diverse range of colors, shapes, and flavors, making them a vital component of a balanced diet; examples include apples, oranges, berries, bananas, and melons, each with unique nutritional benefits depending on the variety
